Fried Rice Recipe

Description:

A close-up, high-angle shot of a vibrant bowl of Nigerian Fried Rice. The rice is a bright, appetizing golden-yellow-green hue, perfectly grainy and non-sticky. It is generously mixed with small, neat cubes of dark brown sautéed liver, tiny pink shrimps, bright orange diced carrots, vibrant green peas, sliced green beans, and golden sweet corn. Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, green beans)
  • 1/2 cup diced liver
  • 1/4 cup shrimps
  • 1/4 cup sweet corn
  • 1/4 cup green bell pepper strips
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Parboil the rice for 8-10 minutes until half-cooked, then rinse with cold water to remove excess starch..
  2. Cook the parboiled rice in well-seasoned meat stock with curry powder and thyme until tender but firm (al dente).
  3. Spread the cooked rice on a tray to cool completely (cold rice fries better).
  4. Heat oil in a large wok or pan and sauté onions, ginger, and garlic until fragrant Add cubed liver and shrimps to the pan and fry until the liver has a light crust
  5. Toss in diced carrots and green beans, frying for 2-3 minutes before adding sweet corn and green peppers.
  6. Working in batches, add the seasoned rice to the pan with the vegetables and proteins.
  7. Stir-fry each batch on high heat for 2-3 minutes to achieve the authentic fried taste
  8. Combine all batches and serve hot.
